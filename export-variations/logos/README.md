# Platform Logos

This directory contains the logo images for all CTV platforms in the export panel.

## Required Logo Files

Place the following logo image files in this directory:

1. **roku.png** - Roku logo (40x40px, on purple #6B2D8B background)
2. **hulu.png** - Hulu logo (40x40px, on green #1CE783 background)
3. **amazon-fire-tv.png** - Amazon Fire TV logo (40x40px, on orange #FF9900 background)
4. **apple-tv-plus.png** - Apple TV+ logo (40x40px, on black #000 background)
5. **peacock.png** - Peacock logo (40x40px, on black #000 background)
6. **samsung-ads.png** - Samsung Ads logo (40x40px, on black #000 background)
7. **roku-channel.png** - The Roku Channel logo (40x40px, on purple #4F1182 background)

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Size**: 40x40 pixels
- **Background**: Logos should work on their designated background colors
- **Quality**: High-resolution for crisp display at 1x and 2x density

## Notes

The logos are displayed with `object-fit: contain` so they will scale proportionally within the 40x40px container while maintaining their aspect ratio.
